Day Descriptions

Day 1

A tour guide will be at 5:30 AM, depart to venue will be at 6:00 AM. The first tour will be at PAG ASA Weather Station in Subic Bay.

Day 2

The first tour will be at First Aviation Academy in Subic Bay. After the tour, they will have lunch at Subic Bay, and then go back to the First Aviation Academy for a seminar.

Day 3

On the third day, students will be visiting the Vessel Traffic Management System at Subic Bay. The tour will start at 9:00 AM, and after the tour, students will have lunch at Subic Bay.

Day 4

The last tour will be at another venue in Subic Bay. After the tour, there will be a short seminar. Students will have free time in the afternoon before heading back.

About

The Computer Science Education Tour offers students and educators a chance to explore the world of computer science through interactive workshops, industry visits, and discussions. Participants will learn about cutting-edge technologies like artificial intelligence, cybersecurity, and web development. They will also visit leading tech companies, research labs, and startups. Networking opportunities will allow participants to connect with professionals and peers in the field. The tour also provides cultural immersion in diverse locations.
